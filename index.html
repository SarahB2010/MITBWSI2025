Skin Cancer Detection with AI

Welcome to our 2025 MIT BWSI project created by Conner, Leyang, Lucas, and Amel. In this project, we will go over Skin Cancer detection.

Overview

We will be using a pretrained CNN model using sample skin lesion data for binary classification (benign vs malignant), while also taking in parameters like age, family skin cancer history, and sun exposure.
